TMS - Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation

My Psychiatrist is accepting new Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation patients. TMS therapy uses highly-focused magnetic pulses to stimulate the areas of the brain known to control mood. The pulsed magnetic field may have a positive effect on the brain’s neurotransmitter levels. Treating depression with TMS therapy provides a breakthrough depression treatment for those who have not been satisfied with the results they have had with antidepressant drugs.
